How to Play:

Play normal black jack just like you would for any other game but before the start of each game, you can choose one of 4 abilities to play with to help you win against the dealer.

sneak: draw 3 cards but only take the 2 most optimal cards

multiplier: double reward on win

reverse: if dealer wins, then you actually win instead

mule: when you lose the round, you don't lose the money you bet
